---
name: Antonov auditorium
---

The Antonov auditorium is located on the ground floor of the Brendan Iribe Center for Computer Science and Engineering.
If you enter the building through the main doors at the north of the building, the auditorium is directly to your left. If you
enter the building through the main doors from the south side, the auditorium is directly across the main floor.  For general 
directions to the Brendan Iribe Center itself please refer [here](https://www.umiacs.umd.edu/about/directions).
